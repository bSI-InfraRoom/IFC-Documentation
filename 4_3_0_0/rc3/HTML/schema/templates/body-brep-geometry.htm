4.8.1.4.3 Body Brep Geometry

The Body Brep Geometry is the representation of the 3D shape of a product by faceted boundary representation models.

The following attribute values for the IfcShapeRepresentation holding this geometric representation shall be used:

  • IfcShapeRepresentation.RepresentationIdentifier = 'Body'
  • IfcShapeRepresentation.RepresentationType = 'Brep'
  • IfcShapeRepresentation.Items = IfcFacetedBrep
