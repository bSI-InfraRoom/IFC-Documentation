Semantic definitions at the entity
Entity definition

The IfcCartesianPointList is the abstract supertype of list of points.

NOTE  The IfcCartesianPointList is introduced to provide a compact representation of potentially larger list of points, such as in point clouds, and in indexable representation of points.

HISTORY  New entity in IFC4.
