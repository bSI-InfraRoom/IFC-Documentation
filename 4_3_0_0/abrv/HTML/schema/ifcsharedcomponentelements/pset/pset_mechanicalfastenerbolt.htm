6.3.4.12 Pset_MechanicalFastenerBolt

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcMechanicalFastener / BOLT

Mechanical Fastener BoltProperties related to bolt-type fasteners. The properties of a whole set with bolt, washers and nut may be provided. Note, it is usually not necessary to transmit these properties in case of standardized bolts. Instead, the standard is referred to.
