4.8.1.3.2 Body SurfaceModel Geometry

The Body SurfaceModel Geometry is the representation of the 3D shape of a product by surface models.

The following attribute values for the IfcShapeRepresentation holding this geometric representation shall be used:

  • IfcShapeRepresentation.RepresentationIdentifier = 'Body'
  • IfcShapeRepresentation.RepresentationType = 'SurfaceModel'
  • IfcShapeRepresentation.Items = IfcTessellateditem, IfcShellBasedSurfaceModel, IfcFaceBasedSurfaceModel
