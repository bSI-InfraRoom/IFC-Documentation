Semantic definitions at the entity
Entity definition

Type of built element in a road or other paved area to provide an even surface sustaining loads from vehicles or pedestrians, usually comprising several courses. NOTE Definition from ISO 6707-1: road, runway, or similar construction above the subgrade.
