Semantic definitions at the entity
Entity definition

The resulting void from modification of existing terrain or road structure by excavation or by other means of removing material. NOTE Definition from ISO 6707-1: void that results from bulk excavation of material. NOTE The material excavated and either used as fill or discarded as waste is not modelled as Cut, but may be handled as a different concept (Resource) in the future.
