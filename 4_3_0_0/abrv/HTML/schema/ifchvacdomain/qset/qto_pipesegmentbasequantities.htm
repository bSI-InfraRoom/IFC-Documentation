7.4.5.24 Qto_PipeSegmentBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcPipeSegment

Pipe Segment Base QuantitiesBase quantities that are common to the definition of all types and occurrences of pipe segments.
Quantities

QTO-XML

NameTypeDescription
Length Q_LENGTH
LengthLength of the segment, calculated at midpoint of cross-section, equal to the distance between inlet and outlet ports.
GrossCrossSectionArea Q_AREA
Gross Cross Section AreaArea of the cross section, including the pipe itself and the interior flow space.
NetCrossSectionArea Q_AREA
Net Cross Section AreaArea of the cross section of the pipe, excluding the interior flow space.
OuterSurfaceArea Q_AREA
Outer Surface AreaTotal area of the extruded surfaces of the pipe (not taking into account the end cap areas), normally generated as perimeter * length.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT
Gross WeightWeight of the pipe segment itself, not including contained fluid.
NetWeight Q_WEIGHT
Net WeightWeight of the pipe segment, including contained fluid as designed.
FootPrintArea Q_AREA Gross area of the site covered by the element.
